## üé® **Brand Guidelines for Pinterest**

## üìà **Success Metrics to Track**

### **Pinterest Analytics:**
- **Impressions:** How many people see the pin
- **Saves:** How many people save to their boards (key metric)
- **Clicks:** How many click through to website
- **Click-through rate:** Clicks √∑ Impressions
- **Engagement rate:** (Saves + Clicks) √∑ Impressions

### **Lead Magnet Performance:**
- **Email sign-ups** from Pinterest traffic
- **Conversion rate:** Sign-ups √∑ Pin clicks
- **Discount code usage:** SAFE10 redemptions from Pinterest
- **Product page views** from pin links

### **Target Benchmarks (Pinterest Averages):**
- Save rate: 1-3% (good), 5%+ (excellent)
- Click-through rate: 0.1-0.5% (typical)
- Engagement rate: 2-5% (good for new account)
