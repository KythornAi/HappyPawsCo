## Generation Tips

**For Google Vertex AI:**
- Copy Primary Prompt directly
- Use high resolution settings
- Generate 2-3 variations, choose best match for brand aesthetic
- Ensure UK home settings are visible (avoid American-style kitchens)

**For Midjourney:**
- Add parameters: `--ar 16:9 --v 6 --style raw` for hero images
- Use `--ar 4:3` for product close-ups
- Add `--s 200` for moderate stylization keeping photorealism

**For DALL-E:**
- Use Primary Prompt, may need to simplify if too long
- Generate in 1024x1024, then crop to desired ratio
- Specify "photorealistic" explicitly
