## Generation Tips

**For Vertex AI:**
- Copy Primary Prompt directly
- Use high resolution settings (1024x1024 minimum, upscale to 16:9 for hero)
- Generate 3-4 variations, choose best
- Request photorealistic style explicitly

**For Midjourney:**
- Use Alternative Prompt if available
- Add parameters: `--ar 16:9 --v 6 --style raw` for hero image
- Use `--ar 4:3 --v 6 --style raw` for section images
- Add `--s 150` for natural photorealism

**For DALL-E:**
- Use Primary Prompt, keep under 400 characters if needed
- Generate at 1024x1024, then crop to desired ratio
- Include "photorealistic" and "professional photography" in prompt
