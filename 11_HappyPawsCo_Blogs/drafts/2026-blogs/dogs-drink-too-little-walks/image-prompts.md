## Generation Tips

**For Vertex AI:**
- Copy Primary Prompt directly
- Adjust quality settings: High resolution, photorealistic
- Generate 2-3 variations, choose best
- All prompts optimised for natural UK outdoor settings

**For Midjourney:**
- Use Alternative Prompt if available
- Add parameters: `--ar 16:9 --v 6 --style raw` for hero image
- Add parameters: `--ar 4:3 --v 6 --style raw` for section images
- Use `--s 250` for moderate stylization

**For DALL-E:**
- Use Primary Prompt, keep under 400 characters if needed
- Generate in 1024x1024, then crop to desired ratio
- Request "photorealistic" explicitly
