## Generation Tips

**For Vertex AI:**
- Copy Primary Prompt directly
- Adjust quality settings: High resolution, photorealistic
- Generate 2-3 variations, choose best
- Look for natural expressions rather than posed

**For Midjourney:**
- Use Alternative Prompt if available (simplified)
- Add parameters: `--ar 16:9 --v 6 --style raw` for hero, `--ar 4:3 --v 6 --style raw` for sections
- Use `--s 250` for moderate stylization

**For DALL-E:**
- Use Primary Prompt, may need to shorten if over character limit
- Generate in 1024x1024, then crop to desired ratio
- Request "photorealistic" explicitly

## Brand Consistency Notes

**UK-Specific Elements to Verify:**
- Car interiors should look British (right-hand drive if steering wheel visible)
- Housing visible through windows should be UK style (terraced, semi-detached, brick)
- Service stations should have British character (green grass areas typical of UK services)
- Weather can be overcast or bright (both authentic for UK)

**Dog Breed Selection:**
- Golden Retriever (hero): Popular UK family dog
- Border Collie: Common UK breed, expressive face for anxiety illustration
- Mixed breed: Inclusive representation
- Labrador: Extremely popular UK breed, good for training imagery
- Spaniel: Classic British breed, perfect for rest stop scene

**Mood Guidance:**
- Hero image: Calm, reassuring, success story
- Anxiety image: Mild concern only, not distressing
- Setup image: Practical, clean, organised
- Training image: Positive, encouraging, achievable
- Rest stop image: Relief, refreshment, practical solution
