## Generation Tips

**For Google Vertex AI:**
- Copy Primary Prompt directly
- Set quality to high resolution
- Generate 2-3 variations and choose the best
- Check for UK-appropriate car interiors (right-hand drive if steering wheel visible)

**For Midjourney:**
- Add parameters: `--ar 16:9 --v 6 --style raw` for photorealism
- Use `--s 250` for moderate stylisation
- For diagrams: `--style raw --s 50` for cleaner results

**For DALL-E:**
- Use Primary Prompt, keep under 400 characters if needed
- Request "photorealistic" or "professional photography" explicitly
- Generate in 1024x1024, then crop to desired ratio
