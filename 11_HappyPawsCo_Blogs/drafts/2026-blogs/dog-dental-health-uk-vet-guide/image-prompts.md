## Image Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1200x800px (landscape) for hero and main images
- **Format:** WebP for web optimisation
- **Style:** Professional, warm, educational photography
- **Setting:** Mix of home environments and veterinary clinic
- **Dogs:** Various breeds including small breeds, seniors, and puppies to represent all audiences
- **Mood:** Reassuring, educational, empowering (dental care is manageable!)
- **Avoid:** Graphic disease images, stressed dogs, overly clinical/scary imagery
- **Tone:** Informative without being alarming, encouraging action through education
