## Email Psychology Principles

### Subject Line Formula
- **Curiosity + Benefit:** "The 3-second check that prevents car disasters"
- **Question:** "Is your dog actually visible at 4pm?"
- **Personal:** "[First name], your basket is waiting"
- **Urgency (gentle):** "Your code expires tomorrow"

### Email Structure
1. **Opening:** Personal, warm, direct
2. **Value:** Tip, story, or helpful content
3. **Offer:** Product or action (soft, not pushy)
4. **Close:** Friendly sign-off
5. **P.S.:** Highest-converting real estate. Use it!

### Conversion Psychology
- **Reciprocity:** Give value first (tips, guides, discounts)
- **Social proof:** "Other pet parents say..."
- **Scarcity:** "Code expires in X days" (honest, not fake)
- **Authority:** Position as expert without being preachy
- **Liking:** Be warm, use humour, be human

### What NOT to Do
- ‚ùå Hard sell in every email
- ‚ùå ALL CAPS or excessive punctuation!!!
- ‚ùå Fake urgency ("LAST CHANCE EVER!!!")
- ‚ùå Generic greetings ("Dear Valued Customer")
- ‚ùå Walls of text
- ‚ùå Multiple CTAs competing for attention
- ‚ùå Em-dashes (AI indicator)
- ‚ùå American spelling
