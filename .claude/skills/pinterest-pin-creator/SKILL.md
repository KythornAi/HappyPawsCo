## Design System

### Pin Dimensions

**Optimal:**
- **Size:** 1000x1500px (2:3 ratio, Pinterest-optimized)
- **Format:** PNG or JPG (PNG preferred for text overlays)
- **File Size:** <500KB (optimized for web)

**Acceptable Ranges:**
- **Minimum:** 600x900px
- **Maximum:** 1000x1500px (Pinterest's recommended)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 2:3 (vertical)

### Layout Structure

**Text Zones:**
- **Top Zone (20%):** Headline area
  - Safe zone: 40px from top
  - Maximum height: 300px
  - Placement: Centered or left-aligned
- **Middle Zone (55%):** Main image area
  - No text overlap (when possible)
  - Focal point for visual
  - Subject placement: Center or rule of thirds
- **Bottom Zone (25%):** CTA area
  - Safe zone: 40px from bottom
  - Maximum height: 375px
  - Placement: Centered

**Safe Zones:**
- **Margins:** 40px on all sides
- **Text safe area:** 60px from edges
- **Logo placement:** Bottom-right or top-right, 40px from edges

### Typography System

**Headline:**
- **Font:** Bold sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins, or similar)
- **Size:** 60-72px
- **Weight:** Bold (700) or Extra-Bold (800)
- **Color:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Placement:** Top 20% of pin
- **Maximum lines:** 2-3
- **Alignment:** Centered or left-aligned

**CTA:**
- **Font:** Medium sans-serif (same family as headline)
- **Size:** 36-42px
- **Weight:** Medium (500) or Semi-Bold (600)
- **Color:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Placement:** Bottom 25% of pin
- **Maximum lines:** 2
- **Alignment:** Centered

**Branding:**
- **Font:** Small sans-serif
- **Size:** 18-24px
- **Weight:** Regular (400)
- **Color:** White or brand color (#A87C5B)
- **Placement:** Bottom-right or top-right corner
- **Minimum size:** 120x120px for logo

### Color Overlays

**Text Background Overlays:**
- **Opacity:** 50-60% dark grey (#000000 at 50-60% opacity)
- **Purpose:** Ensure text readability on any background
- **Application:** Behind all text areas (headline and CTA)
- **Shape:** Rectangular bars or rounded rectangles

**Brand Accents:**
- **Color:** Orange-brown (#A87C5B)
- **Usage:** Logo highlights, CTA box accents (optional)
- **Opacity:** 100% (solid)

**Text Color:**
- **Primary:** White (#FFFFFF) for all overlays
- **Alternative:** Brand color (#A87C5B) for accents only

### Image Guidelines

**Image Types:**
1. **Lifestyle:** Dog in car, winter scenes, travel scenarios
2. **Product:** Flat lays, products in use, gear arrangements
3. **Emotional:** Sleeping dogs, happy dogs, safety scenarios

**Image Quality:**
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1000x1500px
- **Format:** PNG or JPG (PNG preferred for text overlays)
- **Compression:** Optimized for web (TinyPNG or similar)
- **Focus:** Clear subject, good lighting, professional composition

**Image Placement:**
- **Focal point:** Center or rule of thirds
- **Text overlap:** Minimize when possible
- **Background:** Should allow for text overlay readability
- **Subject:** Should be engaging and relevant

**Image Generation:**
- **Tool:** Google Vertex AI (Imagen 4) - current
- **Alternatives:** Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion
- **Prompt Guidelines:** Include "no text" specification
- **Workaround:** Crop or specify "no text" in prompt

## Content Strategy

### Headline Types

**Problem-Focused:**
- Example: "Stuck on the M25 with a Stressed Dog?"
- Use when: Targeting pain points
- Format: Question or statement
- Length: 1-2 lines maximum

**Benefit-Focused:**
- Example: "Keep Your Dog Safe This Winter"
- Use when: Highlighting solutions
- Format: Action-oriented statement
- Length: 1-2 lines maximum

**Product-Focused:**
- Example: "Stuck on the M25 Kit"
- Use when: Showcasing products
- Format: Product name or kit name
- Length: 1 line maximum

### CTA Best Practices

**Guide Offers:**
- Format: "Claim your free guide: [specific benefit]"
- Example: "Claim your free guide: 7 essentials for stress-free UK car travel"
- Be specific about what they get

**Checklist Offers:**
- Format: "Free checklist: [what they get]"
- Example: "Free checklist: What to pack for winter dog car travel"
- Emphasize the value

**Product Offers:**
- Format: "Shop the essentials at HappyPawsCo.co.uk"
- Include clear benefit or discount

**CTA Guidelines:**
- Be specific and actionable
- Include clear benefit
- Keep to 2 lines maximum
- Use active language ("Claim", "Get", "Download")

### Text-to-Image Ratios

**Optimal:**
- **Text:** 25-35% of pin
- **Image:** 65-75% of pin
- **Balance:** Image should dominate, text should be readable

**Acceptable:**
- **Text:** 20-40% of pin
- **Image:** 60-80% of pin
- **Note:** Too much text reduces engagement

### Mobile Readability

**Critical Requirements:**
- Text readable at thumbnail size (Pinterest shows small previews)
- Minimum font size: 36px for headlines, 24px for CTAs
- High contrast: White text on dark overlay
- Clear hierarchy: Headline larger than CTA
- Test on actual mobile device before publishing

## Template Specifications

### Standardized Elements (Template)

**Layout Structure:**
- Vertical format: 1000x1500px (2:3 ratio)
- Text zones: Top 20%, Bottom 25%
- Image zone: Middle 55%
- Safe zones: 40px margins

**Typography System:**
- Headline: Bold sans-serif, 60-72px, white, top zone
- CTA: Medium sans-serif, 36-42px, white, bottom zone
- Branding: Small sans-serif, 18-24px, bottom-right or top-right

**Color Overlays:**
- Text background: 50% opacity dark grey
- Brand accent: Orange-brown (#A87C5B)
- Text color: White (#FFFFFF)

**Branding Placement:**
- Logo: Bottom-right corner, 120x120px minimum
- Tagline: Optional, below logo if space allows
- Website: Include in CTA or as small text below logo

### Customized Elements (Per Pin)

**Headline Text:**
- Problem-focused: "Stuck on the M25 with a Stressed Dog?"
- Benefit-focused: "Keep Your Dog Safe This Winter"
- Product-focused: "Stuck on the M25 Kit"

**CTA Text:**
- Guide offers: "Claim your free guide: [specific benefit]"
- Checklist offers: "Free checklist: [what they get]"
- Product offers: "Shop the essentials at HappyPawsCo.co.uk"

**Image Selection:**
- Lifestyle: Dog in car, winter scenes, travel scenarios
- Product: Flat lays, product in use, gear arrangements
- Emotional: Sleeping dogs, happy dogs, safety scenarios

**Color Accents:**
- Seasonal: Warm tones for winter, cool tones for summer
- Product: Match product colors (e.g., teal for first aid kit)
- Emotional: Warm for comfort, bright for energy

### Three Template Types

**Template 1: Problem-Focused Pin**
- **Headline:** Large, bold, problem statement
- **Image:** Emotional or relatable scenario
- **CTA:** Solution-focused offer
- **Use for:** Pain point targeting

**Template 2: Benefit-Focused Pin**
- **Headline:** Clear benefit statement
- **Image:** Positive outcome or solution
- **CTA:** Specific guide or resource
- **Use for:** Solution highlighting

**Template 3: Product-Focused Pin**
- **Headline:** Product or kit name
- **Image:** Product flat lay or in-use
- **CTA:** Free resource or product link
- **Use for:** Product promotion

## Tools & Workflows

### Image Generation

**Google Vertex AI (Imagen 4):**
- Current tool for custom product/lifestyle photos
- Status: 50 days of free credit available
- Limitation: Imagen loves adding text (workaround: crop or specify "no text")
- Success rate: Very high - professional images on first try
- Prompt tips: Include "no text", "high quality", "professional photography"

**Alternative Tools:**
- Midjourney: High-quality lifestyle images
- DALL-E 3: Good for product photography
- Stable Diffusion: Open-source alternative

**MCP Integration:**
- Image generation MCP: Auto-generate images from prompts
- Template application MCP: Auto-apply branding
- Batch processing MCP: Generate multiple variations

### Design Tools

**Canva Pro:**
- Brand kit: Colors, fonts, logo
- Template library: 5-10 pin templates
- Magic Resize: Adapt to different formats
- Brand folder: Store approved images
- Recommended for: Quick creation, consistency

**Figma:**
- Component library: Reusable pin components
- Design system: Consistent styling
- Collaboration: Team editing
- Recommended for: Advanced design, team workflows

### Automation Potential

- Pinterest posting automation

**Zapier:**
- Trigger pin creation from new lead magnets
- Automated distribution

**API Integration:**
- Imagen → Canva → Pinterest pipeline

## Workflow Recommendations

### Quick Start Workflow (22 mins)
1. **Content Preparation (5 mins):**
   - Write headline (problem/benefit-focused)
   - Write CTA (specific offer)
   - Select image (Imagen-generated or stock)

2. **Template Application (10 mins):**
   - Open Canva template (pre-built with safe zones)
   - Place image in middle zone
   - Add headline in top zone with overlay
   - Add CTA in bottom zone with overlay
   - Place logo in bottom-right

3. **Customization (5 mins):**
   - Adjust text sizes for readability
   - Fine-tune overlay opacity
   - Add color accents if needed
   - Verify mobile readability

4. **Export & Optimization (2 mins):**
   - Export as PNG (1000x1500px)
   - Compress for web (TinyPNG or similar)
   - Add alt text for accessibility

### Template-Based Workflow (15-30 mins)
1. **Template Selection (2 mins):**
   - Choose problem, benefit, or product template
   - Review structure

2. **Content Filling (10 mins):**
   - Add headline
   - Add CTA
   - Select/place image

3. **Design Refinement (8 mins):**
   - Apply brand guidelines
   - Adjust overlays
   - Fine-tune text placement

4. **Export & Review (5 mins):**
   - Export as PNG
   - Test on mobile
   - Quality check

## Pinterest Best Practices

### Algorithm Optimization
- **Vertical format:** 2:3 ratio preferred
- **Text-to-image ratio:** 25-35% text, 65-75% image
- **File size:** <500KB for fast loading
- **Alt text:** Descriptive for SEO
- **Keywords:** In description, not on image

### Engagement Optimization
- **Clear value:** Specific offer in CTA
- **Visual appeal:** High-quality images
- **Readability:** Text readable at thumbnail size
- **Branding:** Consistent across pins
- **Seasonal relevance:** Match current trends

### Mobile Optimization
- **Text size:** Minimum 36px for headlines
- **Contrast:** High contrast for readability
- **Safe zones:** Respect 40px margins
- **Test:** Always test on actual mobile device

## Common Issues & Fixes

### Issue: Text Not Readable
**Problem:** White text on light background
**Fix:** Add 50-60% dark overlay behind text

### Issue: Text Too Small
**Problem:** Headline <60px, not readable on mobile
**Fix:** Increase headline to 60-72px, CTA to 36-42px

### Issue: Image Overlap
**Problem:** Text overlaps important image elements
**Fix:** Reposition text or adjust image placement

### Issue: Branding Not Visible
**Problem:** Logo too small or poorly placed
**Fix:** Increase logo to 120x120px minimum, move to corner

### Issue: File Size Too Large
**Problem:** PNG file >500KB, slow loading
**Fix:** Compress with TinyPNG or similar tool
