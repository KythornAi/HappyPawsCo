## Design System

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- **Background:** Light beige/cream (#F8F7F4)
- **Text:** Dark brown/charcoal (#2C2C2C)
- **Accent Green:** Dark green (#2D5016) - for separators, bullets
- **CTA/Highlight:** Orange-brown (#A87C5B) - for callouts, emphasis

**Usage Guidelines:**
- Background: Consistent light beige throughout all materials
- Text: Dark grey for maximum readability
- Accents: Green for functional elements (separators, bullets)
- Highlights: Orange-brown for important callouts, CTAs

### Typography

**Title Font (Serif):**
- Style: Playfair Display, Crimson, or similar elegant serif
- Size: 36-48px (slide decks), 24-32px (PDFs)
- Weight: Regular to Bold
- Color: Dark brown/charcoal (#2C2C2C)
- Usage: Main titles, section headers

**Body Font (Sans-serif):**
- Style: Open Sans, Lato, or similar clean sans-serif
- Size: 16-18px (slide decks), 12-14px (PDFs)
- Weight: Regular
- Color: Dark grey (#2C2C2C)
- Line height: 1.5-1.6
- Usage: Body text, descriptions, bullet points

**Subheading Font (Sans-serif):**
- Style: Same as body
- Size: 24-28px (slide decks), 18-20px (PDFs)
- Weight: Bold
- Color: Dark grey (#2C2C2C)
- Usage: Section subheadings, emphasized text

### Layout Patterns

**Two-Column Split (Primary):**
- Left Column: 40-45% width - Image zone
  - Full-bleed photography
  - High-quality lifestyle/product images
  - Maintains aspect ratio
- Right Column: 55-60% width - Text zone
  - Light beige background (#F8F7F4)
  - Ample white space (24-32px margins)
  - Clear text hierarchy

**Alternative Layouts:**
- Full-bleed image slides: For cover, emotional impact, closing
- Text-only slides: For checklists, product lists, contact information
- Before/After comparisons: Side-by-side images with labels
- Product showcase: Grid layouts with consistent sizing

### Image Guidelines

**Image Types:**
1. Lifestyle Photography: Dogs in cars, winter scenes, travel scenarios
2. Product Photography: Flat lays, products in use, gear arrangements
3. Before/After Comparisons: Side-by-side with clear labels
4. Illustrations: Simple line-art for technical concepts (minimal use)

**Image Quality Standards:**
- Resolution: Minimum 1920x1080 for slides, 300 DPI for PDFs
- Format: PNG for graphics, JPG for photos
- Lighting: Consistent, professional
- Composition: Clear focus on subject
- Color grading: Consistent across all images

**Image Placement:**
- Left column: Full-bleed, maintains aspect ratio
- Embedded in text: Small circular images with gold/bronze borders
- Product sections: Grid layouts with consistent sizing
- Before/After: Equal-width side-by-side with labels

### Icon Usage

**Icon Philosophy:** Minimal and functional, not decorative

**Icon Types:**
- Checklist bullets: Dark green circular bullets
- Line-art icons: Simple illustrations for technical concepts
- Brand watermark: Subtle paw print (light grey, semi-transparent)

**Icon Placement:**
- Bullets: Left-aligned with text
- Technical illustrations: Right column, above or below text
- Watermarks: Bottom-right corner, subtle

## Content Structure

### Lead Magnet Types

**1. Guides (5-10 pages)**
- Cover page
- Introduction/context
- Main content (tips, strategies, how-tos)
- Product recommendations
- CTA/next steps
- Contact information

**2. Checklists**
- Title and context
- Checklist items (8-10 maximum)
- Empty checkboxes (square, brown outline)
- Optional: Product recommendations
- CTA

**3. Templates**
- Title and purpose
- Fillable sections
- Instructions
- Example usage
- CTA

**4. Quick Reference Sheets**
- Single page or two-page spread
- Key information organized
- Visual hierarchy
- Branding

### Content Organization

**Standard Structure:**
1. **Cover:** Title, subtitle, visual
2. **Introduction:** Context, problem, solution preview
3. **Main Content:** Tips, strategies, actionable advice
4. **Product Integration:** Relevant products with explanations
5. **CTA:** Clear next steps, discount codes
6. **Contact:** Website, social, email
7. **Footer:** Copyright, disclaimer

**Section Templates:**

**Essential Tip Section:**
- Title: "Essential [Number]: [Clear Benefit]"
- Introductory paragraph: Problem/context
- Separator line (dark green)
- Sub-sections with bolded headings:
  - "The Problem:" / "Why It Matters:"
  - "The Solution:" / "What Works:"
  - "Pro Tip:" / "Common Mistake:"
- Supporting image or illustration

**Product Showcase Section:**
- Title: "Your [Topic] Go-To Gear"
- Subtitle: Context/benefit
- Category headers (4 columns)
- Product images with names and descriptions
- CTA at bottom

**Checklist Section:**
- Title: "Your Quick [Topic] Checklist"
- Subtitle: "Before you [action], tick these off:"
- Empty checkboxes (square, brown outline)
- 8-10 items maximum
- Subtle branding watermark

## Template Specifications

### Slide Deck Templates

**Dimensions:**
- Standard: 1920x1080px (16:9 ratio)
- Alternative: 1920x1200px (16:10 ratio)

**Safe Zones:**
- Margins: 40px on all sides
- Text safe area: 60px from edges
- Logo placement: Bottom-right, 40px from edges

**Grid System:**
- Two-column: 40% left, 60% right (or 50/50)
- Three-column: 33% each (for product grids)
- Full-bleed: Image extends to edges

**Text Guidelines:**
- Maximum title length: 2 lines
- Maximum body text: 6-8 lines per section
- Bullet points: Maximum 5-6 items
- Line length: 50-75 characters for readability

### PDF Templates

**Dimensions:**
- Standard: 8.5x11 inches (US Letter) or A4
- Alternative: 8.5x14 inches (Legal) for longer content

**Margins:**
- Top/Bottom: 0.75 inches
- Left/Right: 0.5 inches
- Safe zone: 0.25 inches from edges

**Page Structure:**
- Header: Optional (page numbers, chapter titles)
- Body: Main content area
- Footer: Copyright, website, page numbers

**Text Guidelines:**
- Title: 24-32px
- Subheading: 18-20px
- Body: 12-14px
- Line spacing: 1.5-1.6

### Common Slide Types

**1. Cover Slide**
- Full-bleed image
- Title overlay (centered or left-aligned)
- Subtitle below title
- Logo in bottom-right

**2. Two-Column Content Slide**
- Left: Full-bleed image
- Right: Title, body text, bullets
- Separator line between sections

**3. Checklist Slide**
- Title at top
- Empty checkboxes (square, brown outline)
- 8-10 items maximum
- Subtle branding watermark

**4. Product Showcase Slide**
- Title: "Your [Topic] Go-To Gear"
- Subtitle: Context/benefit
- Four columns: Category headers
- Product images with names and descriptions
- CTA at bottom

**5. Before/After Comparison**
- Title at top
- Two side-by-side images
- "BEFORE" and "AFTER" labels
- Explanatory text below

**6. Contact/Closing Slide**
- Title: "Stay Connected"
- Three columns: Website, Pinterest, Email
- Icons above each contact method
- Footer: Copyright and disclaimer

## Tools & Workflows

### Document Generation Tools

**Document Generator MCP:**
- Generates Word (.docx) and PDF documents directly from Claude
- Installation: `npx document-generator-mcp@latest`
- Use case: Markdown → PDF conversion
- Output: Saves to `./generated_documents/` automatically

**Pandoc MCP Server:**
- Universal document converter (Markdown → PDF, DOCX, HTML)
- Installation: `brew install pandoc` then `npm install -g mcp-pandoc`
- Use case: Template-based conversion with more control
- Output: Multiple formats supported

**Claude Office Skills:**
- Official skills for DOCX, PPTX, XLSX, and PDF creation
- Installation: Clone from GitHub
- Use case: Maximum formatting control with templates
- Output: Professional documents with full control

### Image Generation

**Google Vertex AI (Imagen 4):**
- Current tool for custom product/lifestyle photos
- Status: 50 days of free credit available
- Limitation: Imagen loves adding text (workaround: crop or specify "no text")
- Success rate: Very high - professional images on first try

**Alternative Tools:**
- Midjourney: High-quality lifestyle images
- DALL-E 3: Good for product photography
- Stable Diffusion: Open-source alternative

**MCP Integration:**
- Image generation MCP: Auto-generate images from prompts
- Template application MCP: Auto-apply branding
- Batch processing MCP: Generate multiple variations

### Automation Potential

**Make.com:**
- Auto-generate lead magnets from blog posts
- Template-based PDF generation
- Email delivery automation
- Product link integration

**Zapier:**
- Trigger lead magnet creation from new content
- Automated distribution
- CRM integration

**API Integration:**
- Imagen → Canva → PDF generation
- Content → Template → PDF pipeline
- Batch processing workflows

## Quality Standards

### Design Quality
- [ ] Consistent color palette throughout
- [ ] Typography hierarchy clear
- [ ] Images high-quality and relevant
- [ ] Spacing consistent (24-32px margins)
- [ ] Branding visible but not intrusive
- [ ] Text readable (minimum 12px for PDFs, 16px for slides)

### Content Quality
- [ ] Clear value proposition
- [ ] Actionable advice
- [ ] UK spelling throughout
- [ ] Personal tone ("I've found...", "Many owners...")
- [ ] Product integration natural
- [ ] CTA clear and specific

### Technical Quality
- [ ] File size optimized (<5MB for PDFs)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly (test on phone)
- [ ] All links working
- [ ] Alt text for images
- [ ] Proper file naming

## Workflow Recommendations

### Quick Start Workflow (30-60 mins)
1. **Content Creation (20 mins):**
   - Write content in Markdown
   - Structure with headers and sections
   - Include product recommendations

2. **Document Generation (10 mins):**
   - Use Document Generator MCP
   - Convert Markdown to PDF
   - Review formatting

3. **Design Polish (20 mins):**
   - Open in Canva
   - Apply brand colors and fonts
   - Add images and icons
   - Final review

### Template-Based Workflow (45-90 mins)
1. **Template Selection (5 mins):**
   - Choose appropriate template
   - Review structure

2. **Content Filling (30 mins):**
   - Fill template with content
   - Add images
   - Customize sections

3. **Design Refinement (15 mins):**
   - Apply brand guidelines
   - Adjust spacing
   - Final polish

4. **Export & Review (10 mins):**
   - Export as PDF
   - Test on mobile
   - Quality check

### Automated Workflow (Future)
1. **Content Input:**
   - Blog post or content brief
   - Product list
   - Target audience

2. **Automated Processing:**
   - Content extraction
   - Template application
   - Image generation
   - PDF creation

3. **Review & Publish:**
   - Manual review
   - Approval workflow
   - Distribution

## Common Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Seasonal Guide
**Example:** "Winter Car Safety: 7 Essentials"
- **Structure:** Cover, intro, 7 tips, products, CTA, contact
- **Length:** 10-12 pages
- **Images:** Winter scenes, dogs in cars, products
- **Timeline:** 2-3 hours (manual) or 30-60 mins (template)

### Use Case 2: Quick Checklist
**Example:** "Pre-Journey Safety Checklist"
- **Structure:** Cover, checklist items, products, CTA
- **Length:** 2-4 pages
- **Images:** Minimal, focus on clarity
- **Timeline:** 1 hour (manual) or 15-30 mins (template)

### Use Case 3: Product Guide
**Example:** "How to Choose the Perfect Dog Car Harness"
- **Structure:** Cover, intro, selection criteria, product showcase, CTA
- **Length:** 6-8 pages
- **Images:** Product photos, comparison charts
- **Timeline:** 2 hours (manual) or 45-60 mins (template)
