## The Review Process

### Phase 1: SEO Optimization (Critical)

**What We Check:**

#### 1. Title Tag
- ‚úÖ Contains primary keyword?
- ‚úÖ Under 60 characters?
- ‚úÖ Includes year/modifiers (if relevant)?
- ‚úÖ Compelling and click-worthy?
- ‚úÖ Accurate to content?

**If Issues Found:**
- Current: "Dog Car Safety"
- Suggested: "Best Dog Car Harnesses for Safe Travel (2025)"
- Why: Missing keyword, too short, no value proposition

#### 2. Meta Description
- ‚úÖ Contains primary keyword?
- ‚úÖ 150-160 characters?
- ‚úÖ Includes benefit/hook?
- ‚úÖ Has clear CTA?
- ‚úÖ Grammatically correct?

**If Issues Found:**
- Current: "Learn about dog car safety."
- Suggested: "Keep your pup safe on the road! Our guide covers crash-tested dog car harnesses, installation tips, and top picks for 2025. Read now!"
- Why: Too short, no hook, weak CTA, missing specifics

#### 3. Header Structure
- ‚úÖ One H1 tag (with primary keyword)?
- ‚úÖ Multiple H2 tags (logical sections)?
- ‚úÖ H3 tags where needed?
- ‚úÖ Keywords in headers (naturally)?
- ‚úÖ Scannable structure?

**If Issues Found:**
- Missing: H2 for "How to Choose a Dog Car Harness"

- Missing: H3 under "Top 5 Products" for each product

#### 4. Keyword Optimization
- ‚úÖ Primary keyword 1-2% density?
- ‚úÖ Keyword in first paragraph?
- ‚úÖ Keyword in conclusion?
- ‚úÖ Natural placement (not stuffed)?
- ‚úÖ Semantic variations used?

**If Issues Found:**
- Keyword "dog car harness" appears 2 times in 1,500 words (0.13%)
- Should appear: 15-30 times

- Use variations: "canine car restraint," "dog vehicle safety harness"

- ‚úÖ 2-4 related blog links?
- ‚úÖ 1 lead magnet link?
- ‚úÖ Descriptive anchor text?
- ‚úÖ Links to both dog AND cat content?

- Missing: Links to HappyPawsCo product pages

- Missing: Related blog links
- Add: Link to "Dog Travel Safety Tips" and "Puppy Car Training Guide"

#### 6. Image Optimization
- ‚úÖ Keyword-rich file names?

- ‚úÖ Compressed file sizes?
- ‚úÖ At least 1 image per 300 words?

**If Issues Found:**
- File names: IMG_1234.jpg ‚Üí dog-car-harness-installation.jpg
- Alt text: "Dog in car" ‚Üí "Golden retriever wearing crash-tested dog car harness in back seat"
- Missing images: Add 2 more images (only have 3 for 1,500 words)

#### 7. URL Structure
- ‚úÖ Short (<60 characters)?
- ‚úÖ Contains keyword?
- ‚úÖ Uses hyphens?
- ‚úÖ Lowercase?
- ‚úÖ No special characters?

**If Issues Found:**

### Phase 2: Brand Voice Consistency (Critical)

**HappyPawsCo Brand Voice Rules:**

#### 1. Tone: Warm, Conversational, Empathetic
- ‚úÖ Sounds like chatting with a friend?
- ‚úÖ Empathetic to pet parent concerns?
- ‚úÖ Reassuring and supportive?
- ‚úÖ Never condescending or preachy?

**If Issues Found:**
- ‚ùå "You must always use a harness" (too commanding)
- ‚úÖ "We recommend using a harness to keep your pup safe" (warm, helpful)
- ‚ùå "Failure to secure your dog could result in injury" (fear-based)
- ‚úÖ "A secure harness gives you peace of mind knowing your dog is protected" (reassuring)

#### 2. Language: UK English
- ‚úÖ UK spelling (colour, behaviour, favourite)?
- ‚úÖ UK terms (boot, not trunk; pavement, not sidewalk)?
- ‚úÖ No Americanisms?

**If Issues Found:**
- ‚ùå "favorite" ‚Üí ‚úÖ "favourite"
- ‚ùå "trunk of the car" ‚Üí ‚úÖ "car boot"
- ‚ùå "traveling" ‚Üí ‚úÖ "travelling"
- ‚ùå "sidewalk" ‚Üí ‚úÖ "pavement"

#### 3. Perspective: First-Person Plural ("We")
- ‚úÖ Uses "we" for HappyPawsCo?
- ‚úÖ Uses "you" for reader?
- ‚úÖ Inclusive and partnership-focused?

**If Issues Found:**
- ‚ùå "HappyPawsCo recommends" (third person)
- ‚úÖ "We recommend" (first person)
- ‚ùå "The customer should" (distant)
- ‚úÖ "You might want to consider" (direct, friendly)

#### 4. Personal Experience Claims
**CAN Claim Personal Experience For:**
- Small items (lick mats, puzzle feeders, small carriers)
- Testing processes
- General pet parenting experiences

**CANNOT Claim Personal Experience For:**
- Large items (car seats, large carriers)
- Specific breeds (unless true)
- Medical/veterinary advice

**If Issues Found:**
- ‚ùå "I use this car seat in my SUV" (large item, can't claim)
- ‚úÖ "Based on customer reviews and safety testing..." (honest)
- ‚ùå "My German Shepherd loves this" (specific breed claim)
- ‚úÖ "Many dog owners report their pups feel more secure..." (general)

#### 5. Product Positioning (No Cannibalization!)
- ‚úÖ Each product has unique positioning?
- ‚úÖ No competing claims?
- ‚úÖ Inclusive persona framing?
- ‚úÖ Specific use case for each?

**If Issues Found:**
- Problem: Both harness A and B positioned as "best overall"
- Fix: "Harness A - Best for small dogs" / "Harness B - Best for large breeds"
- Problem: "Only solution for anxious dogs"
- Fix: "Great option for anxious dogs" (doesn't exclude other products)

- ‚úÖ Sources cited where needed?

- ‚úÖ Covers topic thoroughly?

### Phase 4: Readability & Structure (Important)

#### 1. Reading Level
- ‚úÖ Grade 8-10 reading level?
- ‚úÖ Simple vocabulary (no jargon)?
- ‚úÖ Active voice?
- ‚úÖ Conversational tone?

**If Issues Found:**
- ‚ùå Grade 14 (too complex)
- Fix: Shorten sentences, simplify vocabulary
- ‚ùå Passive voice overuse: "The harness should be checked"

#### 2. Paragraph Structure
- ‚úÖ Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)?
- ‚úÖ White space between sections?
- ‚úÖ Not intimidating walls of text?

**If Issues Found:**
- Problem: 10-sentence paragraphs
- Fix: Break into 2-3 shorter paragraphs
- Problem: No white space for 800 words
- Fix: Add subheadings every 200-300 words

#### 3. Formatting
- ‚úÖ Bullet points for lists?
- ‚úÖ Bold for emphasis (not overused)?
- ‚úÖ Subheadings every 300 words?
- ‚úÖ Callout boxes for important info?

**If Issues Found:**
- Missing: Bullet list for "5 Top Harnesses"
- Fix: Convert to bulleted list for scannability
- Missing: Bold on key safety warnings
- Fix: Bold "NEVER leave dog unattended in car"

#### 4. Scannability
- ‚úÖ Easy to scan for key info?
- ‚úÖ Clear takeaways?
- ‚úÖ Visual hierarchy?

**If Issues Found:**
- Too dense - reader can't quickly find "best harness for small dogs"

### Phase 5: Technical Requirements (Important)

#### 1. Word Count
- ‚úÖ Appropriate length for topic?
- ‚úÖ Comprehensive but not bloated?

**Targets:**
- Product reviews: 1,500-2,500 words

- Current: 450 words for product review (too short)

- Add: More product details, comparison, buying guide, FAQ

#### 2. Grammar & Spelling
- ‚úÖ No typos?
- ‚úÖ Proper grammar?
- ‚úÖ Correct punctuation?
- ‚úÖ UK spelling throughout?

**If Issues Found:**
- Typo: "harnes" ‚Üí "harness"
- Grammar: "The harness are" ‚Üí "The harness is"
- Spelling: "traveled" ‚Üí "travelled" (UK)

#### 3. Links Working
- ‚úÖ All internal links work?
- ‚úÖ All external links work?
- ‚úÖ No broken product links?
- ‚úÖ Links open in new tab (external)?

**If Issues Found:**
- Broken: Link to product page 404s
- Fix: Update product URL
- Issue: External link doesn't open in new tab
- Fix: Add target="_blank"

#### 4. Mobile Optimization
- ‚úÖ Readable on mobile?
- ‚úÖ Images not too large?
- ‚úÖ No horizontal scrolling?

**If Issues Found:**
- Images: 2MB each (too large!)
- Fix: Compress to <200KB
- Tables: Break layout on mobile

## Common Issues & Quick Fixes

### Issue: Low Keyword Density
**Quick Fix:**
1. Find 10 places where keyword fits naturally
2. Add keyword to H2/H3 headers
3. Include in first/last paragraph
4. Use in product descriptions
5. Add to FAQ questions

### Issue: Missing Internal Links
**Quick Fix:**
1. Link to 3 related products
2. Link to 2 related blog posts
3. Link to 1 lead magnet
4. Link to 1 cross-species blog (cat if dog blog, vice versa)
5. Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")

### Issue: Americanisms
**Quick Fix - Common Swaps:**
- favorite ‚Üí favourite
- color ‚Üí colour
- traveling ‚Üí travelling
- behavior ‚Üí behaviour
- center ‚Üí centre
- realize ‚Üí realise
- trunk ‚Üí boot
- sidewalk ‚Üí pavement
- apartment ‚Üí flat
- elevator ‚Üí lift

### Issue: Weak Title
**Quick Fix Formula:**
```
[Primary Keyword] + [Benefit] + [Year/Modifier]

Examples:
- "Best Dog Car Harnesses for Safe Travel (2025 Guide)"
- "Cat Carrier Buying Guide: Stress-Free Vet Visits"
- "Indoor Cat Enrichment: 15 Easy Activities"
```

### Issue: No Meta Description
**Quick Fix Formula:**
```
[Hook] + [Benefit] + [CTA] (150-160 chars)

Examples:
- "Keep your pup safe on the road! Our guide covers crash-tested harnesses, installation tips, and top picks. Read now!"
- "Stress-free vet trips start here! Discover the best cat carriers for anxious cats plus transport tips. Shop favorites!"
```

### Issue: Poor Readability
**Quick Fixes:**
1. Break long paragraphs (max 4 sentences)
2. Add subheadings every 300 words
3. Convert lists to bullet points
4. Bold important warnings
5. Add white space

### Issue: Missing Safety Info
**Quick Adds:**
- "Never leave pet unattended in car"
- "Consult vet before [activity] with senior/young pets"
- "Always supervise pet with new products"
- "Check fit regularly as pet grows"
- "Follow manufacturer safety guidelines"
