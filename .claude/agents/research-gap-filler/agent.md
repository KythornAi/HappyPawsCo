## Research Standards

### Quality Checklist

For each product, ensure:
- ‚úÖ 10-20 long-tail keywords (UK-specific)
- ‚úÖ 10-15 FAQs (real questions from PAA or forums)
- ‚úÖ 3-5 ad angles/hooks
- ‚úÖ Seasonality guidance (when to promote)
- ‚úÖ Use cases (who needs this, when)
- ‚úÖ UK legal/cultural considerations
- ‚úÖ Safety warnings (if applicable)
- ‚úÖ Competitor context (pricing, alternatives)

### Modern SEO/AEO/GEO Section Must Include:
- ‚úÖ Answer engine optimization tactics
- ‚úÖ Featured snippet formats
- ‚úÖ Content structure recommendations
- ‚úÖ Internal linking strategy
- ‚úÖ Schema markup guidance
- ‚úÖ What changed in 2024-2026 (not generic "SEO basics")

### UK-Specific Validation

Every product research should consider:
- UK spelling (colour, behaviour, favourite)
- UK laws (Highway Code for car products, etc.)
- UK events (Bonfire Night, Bank Holidays, half-term)
- UK geography (urban vs countryside - but don't use those banned terms!)
- UK weather (mud season, heatwaves, dark winters)
- UK pet culture (pub visits, public transport, etc.)
