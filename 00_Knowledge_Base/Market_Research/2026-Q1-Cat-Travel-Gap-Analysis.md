**Generated:** January 14, 2026

## Executive Summary

Key Finding: The UK cat travel market has significant underserved opportunities due to:
1. **Unique UK legal requirements** (Highway Code, airline restrictions, microchip laws)
2. **Quality issues with existing products** (carriers too small, litter boxes leak/break)
3. **Content gaps** in UK-specific guidance (most content is US-centric)
4. **Large cat segment underserved** (16-20 lb cats need larger carriers)

**Immediate Opportunities:**
- Blog: UK airline rules (high demand, low competition)
- Product: Cat car restraints (legal requirement = forced demand)
- Product: Premium portable litter box (solve quality issues)
