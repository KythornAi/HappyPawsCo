### What We Sell

### How We Talk About Products

- **Specific:** "Hard base prevents sliding" not "amazing quality"
- **Contextual:** Explain WHY it solves a problem
- **Non-Pushy:** Suggest, don't pressure

### Product Integration in Content
1. **Value First:** Give helpful content regardless of purchase
2. **Natural Mention:** "This is where [product] helps..."
3. **Explain Why:** Connect product to specific problem
4. **Multiple Options:** Suggest alternatives when appropriate
5. **Honest Disclaimers:** "If you're on a budget..." or "For serious anxiety cases..."
