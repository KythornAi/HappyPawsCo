## **LEAD MAGNET OPTION 2: Product Selector Quiz Tool**

### **Concept:**
"Find Your Perfect Pet Travel Solution in 60 Seconds"

### **Quiz Structure (5-7 Questions):**

**Question 1:** "What type of home do you live in?"
- Flat/apartment
- Terraced house 
- Semi-detached with garden
- Detached house with large garden

**Question 2:** "What's your biggest pet travel concern?"
- Safety and restraints
- Anxiety and stress
- Mess and cleanliness
- Comfort during long journeys

**Question 3:** "How often do you take car journeys with your pet?"
- Daily (school runs, etc.)
- Weekly (weekend trips)
- Monthly (occasional outings)
- Rarely (mainly vet visits)

**Question 4:** "What's your pet's temperament in cars?"
- Calm and relaxed
- Mildly anxious
- Very stressed/reactive
- Varies by journey length

**Question 5:** "What's your main vehicle type?"
- Small car (hatchback)
- Family saloon
- SUV/estate
- Van/larger vehicle

**Question 6:** "What's your budget priority?"
- Essential safety items only
- Balanced safety and comfort
- Premium solutions for best experience

### **Results Framework:**
Each combination generates personalized recommendations:
- **Primary product** (main solution)
- **Secondary products** (complementary items)
- **Reasoning** (why these work for their situation)
- **Next steps** (implementation tips)

### **Example Result:**
"Based on your answers, we recommend starting with our Crash-Tested Harness System for your anxious medium dog in your family saloon. This addresses your safety priority while the padded design helps with comfort anxiety."

### **Automation Feasibility:** ★★★★★
- Full logic automation possible
- Database-driven recommendations  
- Email follow-up sequences
- A/B testing capability
